AFP Digital Courses - Digital Investigation Techniques (2023)

Certificates of completion

1. Basics

Carry out searches efficiently to find the results you need

Refine searches on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and other social media

Find old versions of online publications and learn how to archive others

Find when a photo or video was first published and who published it

Learn methods for dealing with online harassment and for staying safe online

 

2. Intermediate

Take your searches on social networks to a higher level

Identify clues in images, use online maps and travel through space and time with them

Find witnesses to breaking news events and obtain, check and vet images

Verifying climate claims

 

3. Taking it further

Use different mapping tools and learn how to tackle advanced geolocation challenges
Identify trustworthy sources on health topics and evaluate studies
