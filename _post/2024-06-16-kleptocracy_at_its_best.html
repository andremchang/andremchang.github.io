Reflection | Kleptocracy at Its Best

Posted by Andre in South America on June 16, 2024, and updated on June 16, 2024.

In a kleptocracy,
the corrupt is forgiven,
the official is bribed,
the inept is rewarded,
the politician is idolized,
the honest is punished,
and the citizen forgotten.
Any resemblance to reality
is not pure coincidence.
— Andre M. Chang
